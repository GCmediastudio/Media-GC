The Vision

Photography started for me through a simple moment of curiosity. After watching my brother capture the world through his lens, I picked up his camera one day and instantly felt a connection.

Today, GC Media is defined by a commitment to professional, experimental, and creative storytelling. I look for the angles that haven't been seen and the moments that feel raw and authentic.

Based in the Capital Region of New York, I work locally but am always ready to travel for global bookings.
